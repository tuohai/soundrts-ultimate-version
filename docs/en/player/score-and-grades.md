# Post-game score & letter grades (players)

What the **score breakdown** and **S–E grade** mean after a match.

Formulas and code: [../developer/score-grading-system.md](../developer/score-grading-system.md).

---

## When is it announced?

- **Yes**: custom / random map vs computer; multiplayer (if not spectating)  
- **No**: campaign, co-op campaign; spectating  

---

## What makes up the score?

- **Seven base dimensions** (outcome, mining, efficiency, survival, building defense, combat, demolition) cap at **800** total.  
- **Defeating stronger AI** adds bonus points **on top**, not inside the 800 % denominator.  

You hear each part, then **total** and **letter grade** (S best, E lowest).

---

## Grades and winning

- **Wins** can reach S, A, B, C.  
- **Losses** are **capped at D** — you cannot get C or better on a defeat.  

Some achievements require grade S or A; check the achievement list.

---

## Frugal efficiency

On a **win** with low resource spending, the efficiency line uses **frugal efficiency** scoring and is announced separately.

---

## Achievements

**Vs computer**, achievements read this same breakdown — see [achievements.md](achievements.md). **Multiplayer** announces the score only; no achievement progress.

---

## Campaign

Campaign missions **do not** use this score voice or the same stats recording.
