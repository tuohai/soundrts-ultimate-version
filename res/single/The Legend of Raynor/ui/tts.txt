; === Campaign synopsis (campaign.txt synopsis) ===
7751 Once exiled, Sir Raynor returns to the northern marches at the King's command. From a ruined outpost through twelve brutal battles, he learns to build, charge, and command. When the Chancellor's lies turn lord against lord, Raynor clears the slander with sealed letters, trials of arms, and banners of fealty — until the northern host names him their coalition lord. The capital gates stand open — yet on the eve of victory, one last assassin still waits in the alleys.

The King's messenger finds Raynor — a knight once exiled, now the only soul brave enough to face the dark. "Reclaim the frontier," the messenger whispers. "The realm's fate rests in your hands."
