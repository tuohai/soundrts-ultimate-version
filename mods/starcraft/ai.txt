; StarCraft mod — script AIs for terran / protoss / zerg factions.

; Generic names (peasant, footman, barracks) resolve via faction equivalents in rules.txt.

; Invite "easy" or "aggressive" computers in multiplayer; faction picks the script below.



def sc_terran_easy

watchdog 900

get 6 peasant

get 1 supply_depot

get 1 refinery

get 1 barracks 4 footman

get 1 engineering_bay

get 1 factory 1 tech_lab 1 tank

get 12 footman 2 hellion

goto -1



def sc_terran_aggressive

watchdog 600

constant_attacks 1

research 1

get 4 peasant

get 1 supply_depot

get 1 refinery

get 1 barracks 6 footman

get 1 engineering_bay

get 1 factory 1 tech_lab 2 tank

get 1 armory

get 1 starport 1 tech_lab 1 medivac

get 20 footman 4 hellion 4 tank

attack

goto -1



def sc_protoss_easy

watchdog 900

get 4 peasant

get 1 pylon

get 1 barracks 4 footman

get 1 cybernetics_core 4 archer

get 8 footman

goto -1



def sc_protoss_aggressive

watchdog 600

constant_attacks 1

get 4 peasant

get 1 pylon

get 1 barracks 6 footman

get 1 assimilator

get 1 cybernetics_core 8 archer

get 1 forge

attack

goto -1



def sc_zerg_easy

get 1 overlord
